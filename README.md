# OneFiveFour AI Media Platform

OneFiveFour AI Media Platform is an AI-native operating system for digital publishers.

It manages AI employees, editorial workflows, research, writing, translation, SEO, fact-checking, media generation, analytics, and publishing integrations.

The platform does not replace a CMS. It connects to publishing platforms and acts as the AI workforce behind them.

## First customer

- Razbudise.mk

## Core idea

A human supervises the important decisions. AI employees handle the operational work.
