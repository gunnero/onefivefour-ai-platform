# Vision

OneFiveFour AI Media Platform is an AI operating system for digital publishing companies.

The goal is to let one human manage a complete AI-powered media company. The platform should model the company itself: its organization, departments, AI Employees, assignments, business processes, SOPs, policies, capabilities, knowledge, and performance.

This is not a simple automation platform. The product language should make users feel they are managing a real operating organization with persistent AI Employees, not triggering isolated prompts.

## Operating Thesis

- AI Employees should have durable identities, roles, departments, permissions, capabilities, memory, audit history, and performance records.
- Business work should be represented as Assignments inside Business Processes, not as random prompt runs.
- SOPs and Company Policies should guide repeatable work, approvals, escalation, quality standards, and risk boundaries.
- Brain / Model choices should remain replaceable runtime decisions. An employee can use one model today and another later without becoming a different employee.
- Humans should remain responsible for final editorial, publishing, legal, sensitive, and customer-facing decisions in the MVP.

## Media Company Functions

The platform should allow every department of a media business to be represented by AI Employees:

- Research
- Writing
- Translation and localization
- Editing
- SEO
- Fact-checking
- Image generation
- Video generation
- Social media
- Analytics
- Monetization
- Operations

Razbudise.mk is the first customer, but the platform must be built so future magazines, websites, and external customers can use it.

The platform should not be tightly coupled to Razbudise. Razbudise should connect to the platform through APIs, webhooks, and integrations.
