# Vision

OneFiveFour AI Media Platform is an AI operating system for digital publishing companies.

The goal is to let one human manage a complete AI-powered media company.

The platform should allow every department of a media business to be represented by AI employees:

- Research
- Writing
- Translation and localization
- Editing
- SEO
- Fact-checking
- Image generation
- Video generation
- Social media
- Analytics
- Monetization

Razbudise.mk is the first customer, but the platform must be built so future magazines, websites, and external customers can use it.

The platform should not be tightly coupled to Razbudise. Razbudise should connect to the platform through APIs, webhooks, and integrations.
